O14 Cafe

Where Taste Meets Ambience

O14 Cafe is a cozy, contemporary space in Moratuwa where community and comfort come together. We serve handcrafted beverages, wholesome meals, and sweet indulgences in an inviting environment perfect for study, meetings, or relaxation.

Our menu features freshly brewed coffee, juices, sandwiches, rice bowls, pastries, and seasonal specials — all made with locally sourced ingredients and care.

Whether you're stopping by for a quick bite, meeting friends, or unwinding with a book, O14 Cafe offers a flavorful experience designed to brighten your day.
